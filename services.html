Booking & Studio Policies

We keep things simple and respectful — for your time and ours. If you’re ever unsure about something, reach out and we’ll walk you through it.

Booking

  • How to book: Call or text Chelsey at (509) 666-4050 or Carolina at (509) 230-0881, or email sirenstudio86@gmail.com with your preferred days/times.
  • New clients: Please arrive a few minutes early for a quick consult so we can talk through your goals and any sensitivities.
  • Lash appointments: Arrive with clean, makeup-free eyes for the best retention and to make the most of your time.

Rescheduling & Late Arrivals

  • Rescheduling: If you need to move your appointment, please let us know as soon as possible so we can offer the spot to someone else.
  • Running late? Text or call. We’ll always do our best to accommodate, but your service may need to be shortened so we don’t run into the next client.
  • No-shows: Repeated no-shows may require a deposit to rebook. We want to keep our books open for people who are ready to come in and be taken care of.
